4D MATH AUDIT: Does the scale-invariant cascade make sense?
=============================================================

Question: the cascade is scale-invariant by default (v2.3.1). Does
the 4D math actually work when applied consistently at every level?

ASSESSMENT: The 4D math is INTERNALLY CONSISTENT, with caveats.

1. HIERARCHY CONCENTRATION AT 4D->3+1D
   - Observed: 1e-38 (Planck hierarchy)
   - Per scale-invariance: should distribute across all levels
     (e.g., 1e-19 per level for 2 levels, 2e-13 for 3 levels)
   - The cascade POSTULATES the hierarchy is at 4D->3+1D
   - This is an ARCHITECTURAL CHOICE, not derived
   - Defensible: "the hierarchy is a property of one level,
     not a property of the cascade as a whole"

2. TIME DIRECTION
   - T_4D ~ 1e-21 s, L_4D ~ 1e-12 m (Dark Dimension scale)
   - The cascade's time-dilation rule T_3+1D = T_4D / epsilon works
   - The 4D event is BRIEF, the 3+1D universe is its long aftermath
   - Consistent with current observational constraints on extra dims

3. ENERGY CONSERVATION
   - 32% projects to 3+1D (5% direct + 27% DM)
   - 68% "un-cancelled" is the 3+1D's DE
   - Self-consistent under careful interpretation
   - Energy is conserved (Stoke's theorem in the action)

4. OPEN UPWARD (5D, 6D, ...)
   - Mathematically OK
   - 4D event can be a child of a 5D process
   - Strict scale-invariance requires ~1e-19 hierarchy at each level
   - We can't identify "the" hierarchy-generating level

5. INFINITE REGRESS
   - Physics doesn't require a "first cause"
   - Each level is self-consistent
   - The cascade doesn't claim a "first event"

VERDICT: 4D math is self-consistent, but it has limitations:

1. The hierarchy being concentrated at 4D->3+1D is a POSTULATE,
   not derived. The cascade doesn't say WHY 4D is special.
2. The "4D event" in the cascade is a specific level in a chain
   (per scale-invariance) or the "top" (per cone-shape). Both
   are valid; the choice is architectural (Limitation 11).
3. The 4D event's specific physics (energy, duration, profile)
   is UNSPECIFIED. The cascade has 5+ free parameters (Limitation 26).
4. The cascade doesn't explain WHY 4D is the "top" or why 2D is
   the "bottom" (per cone-shape). These are architectural choices.

BOTTOM LINE: 4D math works, but it's GEOMETRY, not full physics.
The cascade gives the framework; the specific Lagrangian is the
unfinished business (Limitation 26).

OPEN QUESTIONS:
- Is the 4D->3+1D hierarchy "concentrated" or "distributed"?
- Is the 4D event the "top" of the cascade, or a node in a chain?
- What is the 4D event's specific Lagrangian (L_4D)?
- Why is the 3+1D->2D step hierarchy "1" while 4D->3+1D is "1e-38"?

These are all documented in §7 Limitations (1, 5, 6, 11, 26).
