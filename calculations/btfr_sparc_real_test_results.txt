BTFR Test Results (Real Data from SPARC)
==========================================

SPARC sample: 129 galaxies (quality 1-2, Vflat > 30 km/s)
Data: Lelli+ 2016, AJ 152, 157 (VizieR J/AJ/152/157)
M_star from L3.6 with M/L_3.6 = 0.5
M_gas from MHI
M_baryon = M_star + M_gas

BTFR fit (all): M_baryon ~ V^3.53
Expected: M_baryon ~ V^3.5-4.5
Scatter (1-sigma): 0.25 dex

BTFR slope by morphology:
  Early (T<=3): N=26, slope=2.55
  Int (T=4-6): N=47, slope=3.85
  Late (T>=7): N=56, slope=2.84

Verdict: CONSISTENT with both cascade and ΛCDM (NOT discriminative)
  - BTFR is a tight scaling relation
  - Both models predict M_baryon ~ V^4
