HI-richness vs DM Content Test Results (Real Data from SPARC)
=============================================================

SPARC sample: 129 galaxies with HI and Vflat
Data: Lelli+ 2016, AJ 152, 157 (VizieR)
M_dyn at optical radius from Vflat and Rdisk
M_gas from MHI
f_gas = M_gas / (M_gas + M_star)

Correlation by mass bin:
  low M*: N=17, f_gas-ratio r=0.692, f_gas-Rdisk r=-0.040
  mid M*: N=38, f_gas-ratio r=0.560, f_gas-Rdisk r=0.270
  high M*: N=60, f_gas-ratio r=0.653, f_gas-Rdisk r=0.080

Verdict: TIER 2 test, CONFOUNDED by gas-radius correlation
  - f_gas correlates with M_dyn(optical)/M_star
  - BUT: f_gas ALSO correlates with Rdisk
  - The M_dyn(optical) ~ V^2 R / G formula depends on R
  - NOT a clean cascade vs ΛCDM discriminator
