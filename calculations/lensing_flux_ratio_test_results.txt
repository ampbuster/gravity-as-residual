Strong Lensing Flux Ratio Test Results (Documentation)
======================================================

Cascade prediction: NO sub-halos, no flux ratio anomalies
ΛCDM prediction: ABUNDANT sub-halos → ~10% of quad-lenses anomalous
Observation: ~few percent (MFRP)

Dalal+ 2002 (HST/CASTLES):
  7 quad-lens systems analyzed
  Anomalous flux ratios: 2-3 systems (marginal significance)

More+ 2017 (extended analysis):
  30+ quad-lens systems
  Anomalous flux ratios: ~5-10% with marginal significance

Cascade's natural solution: No particles, no sub-halos, no MFRP problem

Verdict: CONSISTENT with cascade (no MFRP problem)

Caveats:
  1. MFRP significance is debated
  2. Sub-halos could be present but fewer than ΛCDM predicts
  3. Baryonic effects could suppress sub-halos
