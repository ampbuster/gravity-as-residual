The "Honest" Picture

The cascade is a geometric framework that a mathematical physicist can develop further. The §2.5.1 action is a skeleton with the right structure (reduces to RS-II when α → 0, preserves local energy conservation by Stoke's theorem), but the specific 2D brane Lagrangian ℒ2D and the coupling α are calibration parameters, not derived constants. The CTP formalism (§2.5.2) handles the creation + destruction symmetrically and removes the teleological issue.

What the cascade does provide:

  • Geometric origin of g₊ (cumulative 2D universe back-projection)
  • Why g₊ is universal at galaxy scales
  • Why g₊ enhances at cluster scales (V_local + MOND EFE)
  • Connection to brane-world physics (reduces to RS-II)
  • Connection to MOND (cascade provides geometry, MOND provides functional form)

What the cascade doesn't provide:

  • Specific 2D brane Lagrangian
  • Derivation of α from first principles
  • Derivation of 5/27/68 from 4D event
  • Resolution of the Hubble tension
  • Derivation of MOND's interpolation function from cascade physics
